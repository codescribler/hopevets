01 — Executive Summary

The Opportunity

Hope Vets has spent over two decades earning the trust of pet owners across Bovingdon, Berkhamsted, Hemel Hempstead and the surrounding villages. Your reputation is built on personal, expert care from a small team who know every patient by name. Your website should reflect that same standard.

Right now, your current site isn't doing your practice justice. It's difficult to update, doesn't present the warmth and professionalism your clients experience in person, and isn't working hard enough to attract new pet owners searching for a vet online.

This proposal outlines how a new, purpose-built website will give Hope Vets an online presence that matches the quality of care you provide — one that builds trust before a client ever walks through the door, makes it easy for them to get in touch, and grows with the practice over time.

What this means for Hope Vets: A site that looks and feels like the practice you've built. One that attracts the right local clients, converts visitors into phone calls, and requires zero technical knowledge to keep current — so you can focus on what you do best.

02 — Understanding Your Situation

Where Things Stand Today

Based on our conversations, here's what we understand about the challenges you're facing and what you'd like to achieve.

Current Challenges

  • The site doesn't reflect the practice. Hope Vets is warm, professional and personal. The current website doesn't convey any of that — it's outdated and generic, which risks giving the wrong first impression to prospective clients.
  • Updating content is a headache. Making even small changes to the existing site is difficult, which means information can go stale — pricing, team details, opening hours. Stale content undermines trust.
  • New clients aren't finding you online. When pet owners in Bovingdon, Hemel Hempstead or Berkhamsted search for a vet, Hope Vets should be front and centre. Without proper SEO and local search optimisation, you're invisible to the people most likely to become long-term clients.
  • No visibility on what's working. There's currently no way to see how many people visit the site, whether they're picking up the phone, or what content attracts them. You're flying blind.

What You Want

  • A website that genuinely represents the quality and personality of Hope Vets
  • The ability to keep key information up to date without breaking anything
  • More visibility on Google for local pet owners searching for a vet
  • A reliable, secure site that simply works — no downtime, no technical headaches
  • A partner who handles the technical side so you don't have to think about it
