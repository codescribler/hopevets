Choosing a vet shouldn't feel like a gamble.

Finding the right vet for your pet is one of the most important decisions you'll make as a pet owner. And with so many practices now owned by large corporate groups, it can be hard to know whether you're getting genuinely personal care — or just being processed through a system.

You want a vet who will actually remember your pet. Someone who takes the time to explain what's happening, gives you honest advice, and is there for you when things get scary. That's not too much to ask. It's exactly what Hope Vets was built to provide.

About the Practice

A practice built around you — not the other way round.

Hope Vets was founded by Stephanie, a Cambridge-trained vet with a GP Certificate in Small Animal Medicine, and her husband Steven. They didn't just open a practice — they designed and built one from scratch in the Hertfordshire countryside, because they wanted it done right.

From day one, the goal was simple: to be the kind of vet practice they'd want for their own animals. A place where the team is small enough to know every patient by name. Where you never have to repeat yourself because the same familiar faces are always there.

Over the years, Hope Vets has become part of the fabric of this community — trusted by families across Bovingdon, Flaunden, Chipperfield, Berkhamsted and Hemel Hempstead.

Our Services

Everything your pet needs, under one roof.

From routine check-ups to specialist treatment, our purpose-built clinic is fully equipped to care for your pet at every stage of life.

Consultations

Whether it's a routine check or something worrying, our vets take the time to listen, examine and advise — with specialist clinics for puppies, kittens and seniors.

Surgery

Our dedicated operating theatre is fully equipped and staffed by experienced, qualified nurses monitoring your pet's safety every step of the way.

Diagnostics

On-site X-ray, ultrasound and in-house lab mean we can often give you answers the same day — without the wait.

Dentistry

From descaling to extractions, we have a full dental suite on site. Because a healthy mouth means a healthier, happier pet.

Physiotherapy

Post-operative recovery and ongoing conditions like arthritis, with referral to specialist physiotherapists where needed.

Hospitalisation

Separate dog and cat wards that are calm, comfortable and staffed by people who genuinely care. Out-of-hours covered by Vets Now, 10 mins away.

Supplies & Prescriptions

Full range of medications, foods and treatments. We're transparent about pricing and keep long-term meds as affordable as possible.

Out-of-Hours Emergencies

If your pet needs urgent help outside opening hours, call our main number and the recorded message will connect you to our out-of-hours service. We partner with Vets Now — a dedicated 24/7 emergency practice staffed by specialist vets and nurses, just 10 minutes away in Hemel Hempstead.

Need advice before you travel? You can also access 24/7 video vet consultations through PawSquad, from £24.
